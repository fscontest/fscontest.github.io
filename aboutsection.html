2025 Rules:

- Select 5 stallions in order of preference. One selection must be a stallion who has under 50 2 year olds per the attached list.

- Entry Fee: $100 to enter, payable by the end of November Sale. Cash, Check or Venmo (available upon request)

- Payment from new players is the responsibility of the person who brings them into the game.

- Statistics as per The Blood Horse website, year-end statistics.

- The prize money will be split as follows: Winner 70%, Second Place 20%, Third Place 10%,

- Last place finisher gets their $100 credited to the next year's competition and the prestigious Joss Collins 'Wooden Spoon' Award.

- Any ties will be settled by using the preference rule as set out below.

- The weighting system for tie break will be the same a total of:

5 times first place rank

4 times second place rank

3 times third place rank

2 times fourth place rank

1 times fifth place rank

Competition Organizers (Geoffrey and Sidney) will make any and all final determinations.
